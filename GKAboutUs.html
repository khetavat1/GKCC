OUR ORIGIN

2010 saw the rise of a new star in Pune’s construction fraternity, our illustrious founder Mr. Gopal Khetavat who took upon himself the task of building a unique legacy of reconstructing the Indian real estate industry. Fired by the desire to create well designed, quality homes for every income bracket, our journey from modest beginnings to a highly reputed company lies in his vision of wholehearted commitment to our valued customers.

‘Quality not quantity’ was our founder’s motto as he strove tirelessly for three decades to keep his customers happy without compromising on any aspect of real estate construction. This belief has become the formidable strength of GK Construction Company that today, places us amongst the best GK construction companies in Pune. We are proud of the immense goodwill and referrals our projects have consistently garnered leading to our growth and success.

Today, Rahul Khetavat and jigar Khetavat, the two dynamic sons who spearhead GK Construction have set their sights even higher. Building on their father’s legacy and immense goodwill, they have put GK Construction on the map by bringing in the latest, contemporary approach to planning, design and project management.
